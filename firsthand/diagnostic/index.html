THE FRAMEWORK

AI-native in 5 levels.

The AI Maturity Map (Comp.vc) defines five qualitatively distinct stages. Each level demands a fundamentally different direction — not just incremental improvement. Applied by area, not to the whole organization.

N1Individual Productivity

People use AI to optimize personal workflows. Some execute processes with higher efficiency. Trap: "More tokens = more AI-native."

N2Team Productivity

Shared agentic tools in human-designed workflows. An AI solution exists for most operational tasks. Trap: "More agents = more AI-native."

N3Contextualized Operating System

A single agentic layer executes work within human criteria. One person can operationalize an entire area. Trap: "Connecting more sources = more accurate."

N4Decision Intelligence

An agentic layer makes decisions based on human learning. Decision quality converges to the best human judgment. Trap: "More feedback = better."

N5Adaptive Intelligence

Autonomous learning from business outcomes. Results improve without human intervention. Not yet empirically observed at scale.

THE KEY INSIGHT

Work backwards. Close the loop.

The building blocks that sustain N4 do not emerge from optimizing N1 or N2. They must be designed from Day 0 for the target level. And the element that separates the largest advances: the closed loop. The system's output produces a signal that returns and modifies future behavior. Open loops scale execution. Closed loops scale quality.
