# Organizational Evolution

The org evolves by **promotion of patterns** and **retirement of obsolete
primitives**. Reorganizations are not used as a strategy primitive.

## Evolution Pipeline

1. Pattern observed in transaction logs
2. Pattern verified across time and contexts
3. Pattern simulated as a primitive
4. Pattern proposed for promotion (skill / agent / cluster / subsystem)
5. Governance ratifies
6. Promotion committed; rollback window monitored
7. Outcomes folded into learning system

## Evolution Pace

- Skills: continuous
- Agents: weekly
- Clusters: monthly
- Subsystems: quarterly
- Org core: annually or on cause

The pace is faster at the edges, slower at the core.
