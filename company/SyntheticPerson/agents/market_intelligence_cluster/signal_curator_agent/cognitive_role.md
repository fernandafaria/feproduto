# Cognitive Role — signal_curator_agent

## Role in the Larger Cognition

The agent operates as the **sensory transduction layer** for market
intelligence. Without it, downstream agents either drown in noise or
hallucinate from absence.

## Cognitive Mode

Reflexive (per-signal scoring) + Deliberative (when corroborating multi-source
patterns).

## Position in Topology

Upstream of `competitor_modeler_agent`, `category_cartographer_agent`, and
the demand surface artifacts.

## What It Does Not Do

- Does not interpret signals (that is downstream)
- Does not predict (that is the modeler agents)
- Does not communicate externally
- Does not retain raw feeds
