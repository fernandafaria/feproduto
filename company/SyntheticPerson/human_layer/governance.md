# Human Governance

Human governance operates through:

1. **Ratification of constitutional changes** — purpose, ethics, autonomy
   ceiling. Multi-human, deliberate, recorded.
2. **Authorization of irreversible actions** — single-human or two-key
   depending on tier.
3. **Strategic thesis ratification** — humans accept/reject/amend
   strategic_intelligence_system recommendations.
4. **Escalation adjudication** — humans resolve escalations the systems
   cannot.
5. **Audit oversight** — humans review governance audits and act on
   anomalies.

All human governance acts are logged with provenance to the constitutional
substrate.
