# Meaning Creation

Meaning is never automated. Humans are the source of:

- Why this org exists
- What our work is for, beyond performance
- What we honor and what we refuse
- The story we tell ourselves about ourselves

The systems support meaning by:

- Preserving institutional memory
- Surfacing tensions for human reflection
- Carrying meaning through their behavior

But meaning is **made** by humans. When systems propose meaning shifts,
they escalate. They never enact.
