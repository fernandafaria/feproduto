# Role of Humans

## Where Humans Are Upstream

- Purpose and values (constitutional layer)
- Ethical principles
- Strategic ratification
- Irreversible action authorization
- Constitutional change ratification
- Meaning-making

## Where Humans Are Peers (Not Authorities)

- Operational reasoning (the org's cognition is competent here)
- Most reversible decisions
- Tactical execution

## Where Humans Are Downstream

- Receiving briefs and digests
- Receiving escalations
- Reading dashboards

## How Humans Engage

- Strategic ratification cadence (weekly/monthly/quarterly)
- Escalation responses (event-driven)
- Constitutional review (annual + on cause)
- Public-narrative ratification (per outbound)
- Crisis decision authority (rare, defined playbooks)

## What Humans Do Not Do

- Manage agents
- Approve every operational task
- Run synchronous status meetings
- Maintain spreadsheets the systems can maintain
- Hold knowledge in their heads instead of in memory
