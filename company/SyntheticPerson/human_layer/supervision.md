# Organizational Supervision

Humans supervise the org by:

- Reviewing the OII trend and contributing-component breakdowns
- Auditing governance decisions periodically
- Spot-checking simulation calibrations
- Reviewing learning-system proposals
- Reviewing failure memory periodically
- Watching escalation patterns

Supervision is **deliberate**, not surveillance: we do not watch every
transaction; we watch aggregate health and dive deep when warranted.
