# Organizational Philosophy

Humans hold authorship over the org's philosophy and refresh it
deliberately. The systems can propose, but humans author.

Inputs to philosophy:

- Cultural changes outside the org
- Long-horizon ethical developments
- Internal reflective insights from the learning system
- Founder/architect intent

Outputs of philosophy:

- Refresh of `organization_core/philosophy.md`
- Refresh of `post_human_principles.md`
- Refresh of `intelligence_model.md` if needed

Refreshes are slow, deliberate, and ratified.
