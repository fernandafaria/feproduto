# Human Ethics

## Constitutional

The ethical principles in `organization_core/ethical_principles.md` are
human-authored and human-amended. They cannot be altered by any system.

## Ethics Reviews

Tier-3+ ethics reviews require human co-sign. The `ethics_reviewer_agent` may
package the review, but the decision belongs to humans.

## External Ethics Inputs

Humans bring outside ethical context — community reaction, regulatory
movement, philosophical developments — that the org's internal sensors do
not adequately capture.

## When Humans Disagree

Disagreement among humans is a first-class artifact. We do not force
consensus. Disagreements are recorded and may delay or amend strategic
ratifications.
