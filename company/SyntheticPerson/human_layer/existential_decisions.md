# Existential Decisions

Reserved exclusively to humans:

- Whether to advance the autonomy level
- Whether to retire or replace nervous systems above tier
- Whether to accept fundamental purpose amendments
- Whether to accept new ethical principles
- Whether to enter or exit jurisdictions, markets, or domains
- Whether to declare org-wide freezes or constitutional pauses
- Whether to pursue or refuse a class of customers/partners
- Whether to release a Synthetic Society of strategic significance

These decisions are not delegated. They are not simulated-as-action — only
simulated-as-evidence-for-humans.
