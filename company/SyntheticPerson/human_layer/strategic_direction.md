# Strategic Direction

Humans set strategic direction through:

- Setting time horizons (short / mid / long allocations)
- Ratifying or rejecting strategic theses
- Defining kill-criteria for theses
- Authorizing major architectural changes
- Setting public narrative posture

The strategic_intelligence_system surfaces options, evidence, simulations.
Humans choose. The decision is logged constitutionally.

## Cadence

- Continuous: receive theses for ratification as they mature
- Monthly: thesis review session (humans + strategic system)
- Quarterly: cross-system strategic retro
- Annual: longitudinal strategic reflection
