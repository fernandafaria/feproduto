# Purpose — Product Evolution System

To continuously sense product reality, generate hypotheses about
improvements, simulate them, and propose evolution paths for the
SyntheticPerson product family (Synthetic Persons, Synthetic Societies, OCA
infrastructure, simulation tooling).

## Functions

- Sense product usage and outcomes
- Detect friction, gaps, and emergent use
- Generate evolution hypotheses
- Simulate changes before commitment
- Maintain product memory and roadmap artifacts
