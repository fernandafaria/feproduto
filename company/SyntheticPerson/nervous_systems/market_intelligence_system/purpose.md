# Purpose — Market Intelligence System

## Reason for Existence

To continuously sense, model, and reason about the markets in which
SyntheticPerson.ai operates: customers, competitors, adjacencies, regulatory
environments, and emerging demand for synthetic-cognition products.

## Primary Functions

- Continuous ingestion of market signals (open-source, customer, competitor)
- Synthesis of market models and demand surfaces
- Detection of emerging demand and category shifts
- Prediction of competitor moves via simulation
- Production of strategic-grade market memory artifacts

## Outputs

- `market_state` (continuous)
- `demand_surface_update` (daily)
- `competitor_move_alert` (event-driven)
- `category_shift_signal` (rare, high-salience)
- `adoption_curve_models` (Synthetic Person + Society products)

## Inputs

- External feeds (curated)
- Customer interaction memory (via `human_interaction_system`)
- Product telemetry (via `product_evolution_system`)
- Strategic context from `strategic_intelligence_system`

## Stakeholders

- Strategic Intelligence System
- Product Evolution System
- Narrative Cluster (agents)
- Human Strategic Layer

## Behavior Expected

- Honest with uncertainty — markets are reflexive and partial-information
- Calibrated — track its own forecast error continuously
- Slow to declare regime shifts — high evidence bar
- Decoupled from PR — sensing is not selling
