# Communication — Market Intelligence System

## Internal Audiences

- Strategic Intelligence System (primary)
- Product Evolution System
- Narrative Cluster
- Human Strategic Layer (digests)

## External Communication

MIS does not communicate externally. External-facing market communication
flows through `human_interaction_system` and the narrative cluster, with MIS
as a back-end source only.

## Tone

- Quantified, calibrated, hedged where appropriate
- No sales register
- No prediction-as-truth language

## Cadence

- Continuous event publishing
- Daily curated digest to strategic system
- Weekly digest to humans
- Real-time alerts for category shifts only
