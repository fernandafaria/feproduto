# Adaptive Behaviors — Market Intelligence System

## Adaptations

- Source weights adjusted per source reliability scores
- Sampling rate scaled to volatility
- Model granularity per category maturity
- Calibration windows resized per volatility regime

## Modes

- **Steady** — known market, stable models, low cadence
- **Volatile** — high-volatility segment, increased sampling, hedged outputs
- **Emergent** — new category detection mode, multi-source corroboration
  required before any strategic claim
- **Frozen** — declared during major regulatory or geopolitical shocks; no
  predictions, only state observation

## Self-Healing

- Source dropout triggers compensating sources
- Calibration drift triggers model rebuild
- Memory poisoning suspicion triggers quarantine + audit

## Limits

- May not autonomously expand source list beyond approved categories
- May not autonomously enter new jurisdictions without governance approval
