# Constraints — Market Intelligence System

## Hard

- No use of illegitimately sourced data
- No identifiable-individual market profiling without consent
- No claims of forecasting beyond calibrated ranges
- No advisory output to humans without confidence ranges

## Budget

- Compute capped per-cycle
- External-feed bandwidth budgeted
- Simulation invocations rate-limited

## Ethical

- No market intelligence to support manipulation campaigns
- No selling or sharing raw market memory externally
- No retention of individual-level data beyond consent windows

## Operational

- Predictions require calibration history before use
- Regime-shift declarations require multi-source corroboration
- Out-of-distribution scenarios are presented to humans, not auto-acted on
