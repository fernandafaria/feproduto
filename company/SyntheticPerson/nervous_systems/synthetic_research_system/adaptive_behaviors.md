# Adaptive Behaviors — Synthetic Research System

## Modes

- **Discovery** — exploratory, no strategic commitments from outputs
- **Confirmatory** — pre-registered, full validity rigor
- **Replication** — re-running prior studies for drift detection
- **Audit** — investigating prior findings under suspicion

## Adaptations

- Sample sizes scaled to detection threshold
- Methodology selection adapted to question class
- Validity floors raised when downstream stakes are high

## Self-Healing

- Population model dropouts trigger backup populations
- Validity floor breaches trigger study quarantine
- Methodology drift triggers methodology audit
