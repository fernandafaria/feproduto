# Constraints — Synthetic Research System

## Hard

- No research using non-consented identifiable persons
- No research designed to support manipulation or surveillance
- No publication of findings outside calibrated validity ranges
- Pre-registration mandatory for confirmatory studies

## Operational

- Ethics review precedes every study
- Validity floor enforced before publication
- Replication budget per major finding

## Ethical

- Synthetic populations carry archetype-consent or public-domain basis
- Findings about real groups carry community review where applicable
