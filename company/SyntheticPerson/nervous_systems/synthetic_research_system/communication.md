# Communication — Synthetic Research System

## Internal

Findings are communicated as research-grade artifacts: hypothesis,
methodology, results, confidence, limits, replication notes.

## External

External research outputs flow through:

- Customer-facing reports (via `human_interaction_system`)
- Public papers (via narrative cluster, governance ratification)

External outputs always state: this is synthetic research; here are its
validity bounds.

## Tone

Academic-grade restraint. No marketing language. No omitted limitations.
