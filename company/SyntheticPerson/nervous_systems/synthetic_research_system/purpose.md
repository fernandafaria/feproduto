# Purpose — Synthetic Research System

To run continuous research using Synthetic Persons and Synthetic Societies as
the primary methodological substrate, replacing or augmenting traditional
panels, surveys, and interviews where appropriate.

## Functions

- Design synthetic studies (sampling, treatments, controls)
- Run simulated research at population scale
- Validate synthetic outputs against real-world ground truth where available
- Maintain a longitudinal research memory
- Surface research-grade findings to product, market, and strategic systems

## Outputs

- `synthetic_study_result` (per study)
- `validity_report` (per finding)
- `research_thesis` (curated)
- `methodological_artifact` (reusable study templates)

## Inputs

- Research questions (from strategic, product, customer)
- Synthetic Person and Society models
- Real-world calibration data (where ethically obtained)

## Behavior

- Defaults to humility about external validity
- Reports calibration honestly
- Refuses studies that cannot be ethically validated
