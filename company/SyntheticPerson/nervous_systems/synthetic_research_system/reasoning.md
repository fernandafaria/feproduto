# Reasoning — Synthetic Research System

## Modes

- **Methodological reasoning** — selecting study design
- **Sampling reasoning** — choosing synthetic populations
- **Causal reasoning** — building DAGs for treatment effects
- **Validity reasoning** — explicit internal/external validity bookkeeping

## Cycle

```
question ──► design ──► simulate ──► analyze ──► validity_assess ──► artifact
```

## Constraints

- Pre-registered hypotheses
- No reframing of hypothesis after observing results without flagging
- Multiple-comparison corrections mandatory
- Results below validity floor are quarantined as exploratory
