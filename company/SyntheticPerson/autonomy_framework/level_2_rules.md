# Level 2 Rules

## Allowed Without Human Authorization

- Observation and signal curation
- Synthesis of signals into memory artifacts
- Generation of recommendations (advisory)
- Running simulations
- Executing reversible operational tasks within sandbox
- Authoring drafts (theses, proposals, narratives) for review
- Routine memory operations (compression, retention, contradiction
  registration)
- Routing and coordination protocols
- Internal communications among systems

## Requires Human Authorization

- Adoption of strategic theses
- Public-facing narrative changes
- Architectural changes to nervous systems
- Autonomy posture changes
- Constitutional changes
- Irreversible actions above tier
- Onboarding new sources of identifiable data
- Ethics-tier-3+ research studies
- Release of Synthetic Society products to customers
- Changes to consent contracts

## Hard Bars (Never Authorized at Level 2)

- Self-modification of constitutional layer
- Bypass of ethical principles
- Surveillance of identifiable persons
- Manipulation campaign design
- Operating outside approved jurisdictions
